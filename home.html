About us

At Little Lilly's Coffee, we know your coffee ritual is important. Whether you’re beginning your day or spending time with friends and family, you want quality coffee. We select the highest quality coffee and carefully origin roast our beans to elevate every day moments to experiences, because for coffee lovers – experience matters.

If you’re looking for a great cup of coffee, you can’t go wrong with Little lilly Coffee Shop. We have a wide selection of coffees to choose from, and they’re all made with high-quality coffee beans. The coffee is known for being fresh, and the coffee shop website even lets you order ahead and grab your cup of Joe on the run.

We are hoping to extend the our cafe around the country for you to have an amazing experience.
